Data Privacy Agreement

This agreement covers how POLYTECHNIC UNIVERSITYR OF THE PHILIPPINES - TAGUIG handles motorcycles and
student information for parking purposes. By using school parking facilities, students agree to provide
necessary information for parking management. This includes motorcycle details and student identification.
The school will protect this information and only use it for parking-related purposes, ensuring security
and efficiency. Students have rights over their data, including access, rectification, and deletion as
needed. Any changes to this agreement will be communicated, and continued use of parking facilities implies
acceptance. By using school parking facilities, students acknowledge and agree to this Data Privacy
Agreement in accordance to Republic Act No.10173 - Data Privacy Act 2012.
